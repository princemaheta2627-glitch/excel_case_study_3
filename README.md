# 📌 Project Overview

The **SweetFoot.Co Retail Sales Analytics** project is an advanced Microsoft Excel case study that analyzes one month of retail sales transactions to evaluate business performance using **PivotTables**, **PivotCharts**, **Slicers**, and **Excel formulas**.

The project transforms raw transaction data into an interactive analytical dashboard that identifies the **best-performing employee**, **highest revenue-generating product**, **average transaction value**, and **sales trends** across products and weekdays.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze monthly retail sales performance
- Calculate key business KPIs
- Identify top-performing sales employees
- Evaluate product-wise revenue contribution
- Analyze daily sales trends
- Build an interactive Excel dashboard
- Present actionable business insights

# 📈 Employee Performance Analysis

| Rank | Employee | Revenue | Contribution |
|------|----------|----------|--------------|
| 🥇 1 | Naitik | **$4,590** | **36.3%** |
| 🥈 2 | Vishwas | **$4,050** | **32.0%** |
| 🥉 3 | Daksh | **$4,010** | **31.7%** |

### Key Insight

- **Naitik** generated the highest total revenue.
- **Vishwas** recorded the highest average transaction value, indicating stronger upselling performance.
- Revenue distribution across employees remained balanced.

---

# 👟 Product Performance

| Product | Revenue | Contribution | Units Sold |
|----------|----------|--------------|------------|
| Shoes | **$7,130** | **56.4%** | 57 |
| Slippers | **$3,430** | **27.1%** | 50 |
| Sandals | **$2,090** | **16.5%** | 33 |

### Key Insight

- Shoes generated the highest revenue despite similar sales volume.
- Slippers had strong sales volume but lower average selling price.
- Sandals contributed the least revenue.

---

# 📅 Day-wise Sales Analysis

| Day | Revenue | Contribution |
|-----|----------|--------------|
| Saturday | **$2,855** | **22.6%** |
| Friday | **$2,620** | **20.7%** |
| Tuesday | **$2,115** | **16.7%** |
| Monday | **$1,905** | **15.1%** |
| Thursday | **$1,610** | **12.7%** |
| Wednesday | **$1,545** | **12.2%** |

### Key Insight

- Friday and Saturday generated over **43%** of monthly revenue.
- Wednesday recorded the lowest sales, highlighting an opportunity for promotional campaigns.

# 💡 Business Insights

- Average transaction value: **$191.67**
- Shoes contributed more than half of total revenue.
- Naitik was the highest revenue-generating salesperson.
- Vishwas achieved the highest average transaction value.
- Weekend sales significantly outperformed weekdays.
- Revenue distribution across employees remained balanced.
